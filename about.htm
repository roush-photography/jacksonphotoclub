About Us

 

The Jackson Photo Club is an organization for Beginner, Amateur, Pro-Amateur, and Pro Photographers.
Our goal with our club is to foster education through pro-grams, seminars, lectures, field trips, and photo safaris.
We have monthly Photography Programs at our meetings to help each person learn new things.

At each monthly meeting we also host a photography contest among the members with judging and winners.
The Jackson Photo Club meets on the 2nd Tuesday of each month at Jackson State Community College / Classroom Building / Basement
We often hold extra events where we travel as a group to a location specifically for the purpose of doing photogra-phy and learning new photo tricks.

Events might include traveling to Reelfoot Lake to photograph eagles or to Middle Tennessee to photograph water falls.
Comradery, Friendship, and Education are the foundation of this organization.
